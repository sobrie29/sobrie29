Manufacturing Partners

Evergreen Valley Foods
Ready-to-Eat Meals & Frozen Entrées

Project: Packaging Line Automation & Controls Modernization

Tradewinds partnered with Evergreen Valley Foods to modernize an aging packaging line supporting their growing ready-to-eat product portfolio. The project included PLC upgrades, standardized HMI screens, and enhanced changeover logic to support frequent SKU transitions.

Impact:
  • Increased line throughput by over 20%
  • Reduced operator errors through intuitive HMI design
  • Improved reliability and reduced unplanned downtime
Redwood Creamery Co.
Cultured Dairy & Specialty Yogurt Products

Project: Process Automation & CIP Improvements

Tradewinds supported Redwood Creamery with automation improvements to batch processing and CIP systems, focusing on recipe consistency, automated sequencing, and improved operator visibility.

  • Strengthened food safety and sanitation compliance
