Industry Partners

Tradewinds Automation and Engineering collaborates with food manufacturers and automation technology providers to deliver practical, scalable solutions. Our partnerships span packaging, process systems, maintenance platforms, and industrial modernization initiatives.

Manufacturing Partners

Evergreen Valley Foods
Tradewinds supported Evergreen Valley Foods with a comprehensive packaging line automation project, including PLC upgrades, standardized HMI development, and improved changeover logic to support frequent SKU transitions.

The project resulted in higher line throughput, improved operator confidence, and reduced unplanned downtime during peak production periods.

Redwood Creamery Co.
For this cultured dairy producer, Tradewinds delivered process automation enhancements across batch handling and CIP systems. Improvements focused on repeatability, sanitation sequencing, and operator visibility into critical process parameters.

The outcome was improved product consistency, reduced CIP cycle times, and stronger alignment with food safety requirements.

Harvest Field Bakery
Tradewinds partnered with Harvest Field Bakery to implement a CMMS and establish a structured maintenance strategy across production assets.

This engagement improved preventive maintenance compliance, reduced reactive maintenance events, and provided leadership with clearer insight into asset reliability and maintenance costs.

Equipment & Technology Partners

FlowCore Technologies — Valves and Process Pumps
Tradewinds works closely with FlowCore Technologies to integrate hygienic valves and pumps into automated food and CPG process systems. Collaboration spans equipment selection, control strategy alignment, and commissioning support.

Together, we deliver reliable, maintainable systems that perform as designed under real production conditions.

SignalPath Instrumentation — Sensors and Measurement Devices
Through our partnership with SignalPath Instrumentation, Tradewinds deploys standardized sensor solutions for pressure, temperature, flow, and level measurement.

This partnership improves data accuracy, simplifies troubleshooting, and strengthens process control across manufacturing operations.

PlantView Software — Automation Visualization Platforms
PlantView Software partners with Tradewinds to modernize operator interfaces and visualization systems for both new and legacy automation platforms.

The result is intuitive, scalable HMI and SCADA systems that improve usability, alarm management, and long-term system maintainability.
